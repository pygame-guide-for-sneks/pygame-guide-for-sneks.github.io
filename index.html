Introduction

"Where can I learn how to use pygame?" - one of the most commonly asked questions that I get. But I always struggle to answer, I sometimes point them to the docs and examples, but that isn't a comprehensive guide on building good pygame based applications. There are many other tutorials and guides I found, but I always find something lacking, there are many comments we give and many nitpicks we have - so much so that some of us gathered and decided to put all of them into a book. This book.
